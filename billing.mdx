## Budget Alert

Set spending limits, get notified as you approach them, and optionally auto-suspend services when a budget is exceeded

**Budget Alerts** is a Billing feature that lets you set a spending limit for your Q0 account and get notified as your usage approaches it — before a bill surprises you.

Without a budget alert, the only way to know how much you're spending is to check the Billing dashboard yourself. With one configured, Q0 tracks your spend against the limit you set and proactively surfaces warnings as you approach it — and, optionally, can stop billable services automatically once you cross it.

<Frame>
  ![Image](/images/image-103.png)
</Frame>

Set a spending ceiling on either a **Daily** or **Monthly** cadence, in ₹.

- **Get notified at meaningful checkpoints** before you hit the limit, using sensible defaults (50% / 70% / 90%) or your own custom thresholds.

- **Auto-suspend services** once the budget is exceeded, so an unattended job or runaway usage can't quietly overrun your limit.

- **Track multiple budgets at once** — e.g. a Daily budget as a guardrail alongside a Monthly budget for overall planning.

- **See spend-vs-budget at a glance** for every configured alert, with a live progress indicator.

 Once your Monthly usage crosses a configured threshold, Q0 shows a **banner at the top of the application** — visible across pages, not just on the Billing screen — calling out which budget triggered it and how much of it you've used
