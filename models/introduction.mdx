Welcome to the Models section. Here you can track, version, and manage all your AI models.

**My Models** is Q0's model registry — a dedicated space where users can register, configure, and manage their own AI models, separate from the pre-built models available in the Marketplace. Whether the model lives on Huggingface, a cloud storage bucket, a private Docker container registry, or Azure Blob Storage, My Models gives users a consistent workflow to connect the model to Q0's infrastructure, optimize it for performance, and prepare it for deployment, fine-tuning, or benchmarking.

## Key Features

> **Bring Your Own Model**: Register any model — fine-tuned checkpoints, proprietary models, or open-source models hosted on external platforms — and run it on Q0's GPU infrastructure without rebuilding your data pipeline

> **Centralized Model Registry**: All user-registered models are tracked in one place with their source, model class, quantization configuration, infrastructure details, and current status

> **Optimize Before Deploying**: Apply quantization schemes (F16, BITSANDBYTES etc.) to reduce model memory footprint and improve inference speed before the model is deployed to production

> **Connect Across Workflows**: Once registered, a My Models model becomes available as a **Custom Model** option inside Deployments, Benchmarking, and Batch Inference — just like Marketplace models, but using your own weights

> **Monitor Processing Status**: Track the model registration pipeline from acceptance through optimization to readiness in real time, with a detailed Logs tab for debugging

> **Deploy Directly**: From the model's detail page, navigate directly to Deployments to serve the model on a live inference endpoint
