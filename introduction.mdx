Fine-tune and train models with Q0's high-performance infrastructure.

Training Module is Q0's built-in fine-tuning workbench. It lets you take a pretrained base model and adapt it to your own data and use case, entirely through a guided 7-step wizard — no separate training infrastructure or scripts to manage.

The Training section provides tools and resources to help you build better models.

## Training Capabilities

> **Fine-tune on your own data** — attach an existing dataset or upload a new one and train a base model to better fit your domain, tone, or task

> **Choose your training approach** — full fine-tuning or parameter-efficient methods depending on the model category

> **Go beyond supervised fine-tuning** — for LLMs and Diffusion Models, run preference-based tuning (RLHF via DPO) on top of an already fine-tuned checkpoint to better align outputs with human preferences

> **Control the full training recipe** — learning rate, batch size, schedulers, regularization, and dozens of model-specific parameters are all exposed, not hidden behind defaults

> **Scale training to your workload** — run on a single GPU or distribute across multiple nodes/GPUs using DDP, DeepSpeed, or FSDP, on H100 or L40S infrastructure provisioned through your Yotta Infrastructure Account

> **Validate before you trust it** — built-in evaluation options (auto-split, custom validation set, or skip) plus category-specific metrics let you measure whether the fine-tune actually improved on the base model

> **Prepare the model for production** — optional quantization (AWQ, F16, etc.) at the end of training tailors the output checkpoint for efficient deployment

> **Track every run in one place** — the My Training dashboard lists every job with its status, dataset, and base model, and each job's detail page shows live training progress, logs, usage, and a one-click path to deployment once training completes
